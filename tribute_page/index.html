Tribute to Sanduk Ruit

Introduction

Sanduk Ruit is a renowned Nepali ophthalmologist who has dedicated his life to restoring vision for thousands of people suffering from cataracts. Born in a remote village in Nepal, Dr. Ruit's journey from humble beginnings to becoming a global leader in eye care is nothing short of extraordinary.

Achievements

  • Co-founded the Himalayan Cataract Project, providing high-quality eye care in underserved regions.
  • Pioneered the small-incision cataract surgery (SICS) technique, making cataract treatment more affordable and accessible.
  • Performed over 100,000 cataract surgeries, restoring sight to people in Nepal and various countries around the world.
  • Received numerous awards and recognition, including the Ramon Magsaysay Award and the Right Livelihood Award.

Legacy

Dr. Sanduk Ruit's legacy extends far beyond his surgical skills. He has inspired a new generation of eye care professionals, empowering them to carry on his mission and make eye care accessible to all. His humanitarian work has touched the lives of countless people, leaving a lasting impact on global health and well-being.

Memorable Quote

"Blindness is not a cause; it is an effect. The real cause is poverty."
